About Me
Hi, I'm Robert!

An Arizona native looking for a career change into the IT field. I have always been interested in computers and anything technology ever since my mom sent me off to computer camp for a week back when I was younger. Since then, I have earned an associate degree in Graphic Design and do a little freelance work from time to time. After the last five years in healthcare as a Specimen Accessioner, I feel I have gained valuable professional experience in time management, attention to detail, problem solving, and being a team player. I am always open to challenges and constructive feedback. I am open-minded and learn new things quickly.

My why is to inspire my family and others to follow their dreams in life, to bring inspiration, and to empower them to achieve more.

Over the last few years, I have taken a few college courses as well as self-study courses online in Web Development. Currently I am enrolled in the New Apprenticeship IT Service Now Bootcamp program where I aspire to become a Certified Systems Administrator.
